How to Play

  1. Pick a Driver & Kart. Build a 40 card deck.
  2. Pick 10 Utilities and 10 Items, and fill the rest with Fuel
  3. Spend Fuel to play Items/Utilities. Dodge, hit, overtake.
  4. Highest position at race end wins. Simple—brutal—fun.
